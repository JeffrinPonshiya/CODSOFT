Early Life

Ratan Tata was born on December 28, 1937, in Mumbai, India, into the prominent Tata family. After his parents separated when he was ten, he and his younger brother were raised by their grandmother, Lady Navajbai Tata, who played a significant role in shaping his values and character.

He attended the Campion School in Mumbai and later the Cathedral and John Connon School. Ratan Tata then moved to the United States to study architecture and structural engineering at Cornell University, where he developed a keen interest in design and innovation.

After completing his undergraduate studies, he worked briefly with Jones and Emmons in Los Angeles before returning to India. He later attended the Advanced Management Program at Harvard Business School in 1975, which further broadened his perspective on business and leadership.

These formative years, marked by both privilege and personal challenges, instilled in him a deep sense of responsibility, resilience, and a passion for excellence that would define his future leadership.
